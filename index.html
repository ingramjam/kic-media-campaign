Video Creative Requirements
Vertical Video (9:16)
  • Duration: 15-60 seconds
  • Format: MP4 or MOV
  • Resolution: 1080x1920px minimum
  • File size: <100MB
  • Frame rate: 30fps or 60fps
  • Captions: Required (auto or burned-in)
Image Creative Requirements
Static Images
  • Format: JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP
  • Resolution: 1080x1080px (square) recommended
  • File size: <30MB
  • Text overlay: 20% of image max
  • Color mode: RGB
Audio/Voice-Over Notes
Audio Requirements
  • Format: MP3, WAV, AAC
  • Sample rate: 44.1kHz or 48kHz
